about

Udayan is a Carbon-based, aspiring front-end web developer, who dabbles in standup and writing. You may have heard of a little novel called "The Stranger." Well he didn't write that, Camus did. But it's fantastic and he's read it if that counts for something.

He (Udayan, not Camus) has a degree in Biology, which he has yet to use in any capacity other than pure appreciation. And he has a webcomic here, which he updates whenever he remembers he's still paying for the hosting.

Udayan is currently enrolled in life and loves to learn.

Look on his works, ye Mighty, and despair. Or, just kind of go "heh, nice."
