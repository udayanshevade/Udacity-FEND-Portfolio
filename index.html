about

Udayan is a Carbon-based, aspiring front-end web developer, who dabbles in standup and writing. You may have heard of a little novel called "The Stranger." Well he didn't write that, Camus did. But it's fantastic and he's read it. His hope is to write a published story soon.

He (Udayan, not Camus) has a degree in Biology and enjoys all things scientific. Darwin is his patron saint. He updates a webcomic here as often as he can.

Udayan is currently enrolled in life and loves to learn.

Look on his works, ye Mighty, and despair. Or, just go "heh, nice."
